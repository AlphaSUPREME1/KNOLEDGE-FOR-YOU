Time Management for Students

Stay productive with these simple strategies:

  1. Create a daily schedule and prioritize tasks.
  2. Use timers to focus on one task at a time.
  3. Set realistic goals for each day.
  4. Avoid procrastination by eliminating distractions.
  5. Review your progress every evening.
