5 Study Tips for Class 10 Students

Boost your exam performance with these tips:

  1. Make a study timetable and stick to it.
  2. Take short breaks every hour.
  3. Revise old topics before learning new ones.
  4. Practice previous exam papers.
  5. Keep notes organized and neat.
